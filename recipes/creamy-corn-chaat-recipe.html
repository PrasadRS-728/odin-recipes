Creamy Corn Chaat Recipe

Description

Corn Chaat is a common snack that is loved by people of all ages, be it kids or adults. f you often avoid fried and oily snacks, then this Corn Chaat is perfect for you. Loaded with veggies like bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes and corn, this recipe will be an instant hit.

Ingradients

  • 1 cup boiled american corn kernels
  • 5 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 slice lemon slices
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  • 2 tablespoon sev
  • 2 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup cubed zucchini
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon chilli flakes
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoon garlic mayonnaise
  • salt as required

Steps

  1. Salute The Veggies

    2. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped zucchini and red bell pepper. Saute for 1 min on medium flame. Now add corn and roast for another minute

  2. Add The Seasoning

    3. Now add red chilli powder, chilli flakes and salt as per taste. Give a good mix.

  3. Garnish & Serve

    4. Take out the mixture in a bowl. Garnish with cherry tomatoes, lemon, coriander leaves, sev and garlic mayo. Give a toss and serve.
