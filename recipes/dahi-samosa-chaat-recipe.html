Dahi Samosa Chaat

Description

If you are in the mood for some spicy chaat, then we have a special and exclusive recipe for you. Try our all time favourite Dahi Samosa Chaat, which is prepared using super yummy and delicious samosas along with hung curd.

If you have a kitty party to throw the next weekend, a birthday party or even if you are having a gala time with your friends enjoying the potluck, this Dahi Samosa Chaat recipe will be a super hit among all your family members and friends Do try making this chaat and let's know if you enjoyed it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds
  • 3 mashed,peeled potato
  • 3/4 gm coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • refined oil as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • tamarind paste as required
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon butter
  • salt as required
  • 3/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 medium onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds/li>
  • 2 dashes black salt
  • 3 1/2 cup hung curd
  • sev as required
  • 2 tablespoon cut into strips beetroot
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoon cut into strips carrot

Steps

  1. Knead the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes

    2. To prepare this sumptuous chaat recipe, mix together all purpose flour, salt, butter and carom seeds with a little water to make a smooth dough. Give the kneaded dough a resting time of about 15 to 20 minutes.

  2. Prepare the filling for samosa

    3. Blanch the peas for about 2 minutes while you microwave the potatoes. After the potatoes are microwaved, mash and peel them. Preheat some oil in a pan and saute chopped onions and all the spices. Then add blanched peas and mashed potatoes to it. Mix the whole mixture over a medium to high flame. After the mixture is incorporated, remove from heat and allow it to cool down

  3. Deep fry the samosas

    4. In a heavy deep-bottomed pan, preheat the refined oil for deep frying. Now, pull a portion from the kneaded dough and roll it to form a smooth flat circle. With the help of a spoon, place the appropriate amount of the filling in the middle of the circle and envelope the filling with the dough in the desired shape. Seal all the sides and carefully drop them into hot oil to deep fry.

  4. Prepare the Dahi Samosa Chaat

    5. Beat the hung curd with some sugar and red chilli powder. Transfer the fried samosas to a plate and break it into many pieces. Pour the hung curd over the samosas and add tamarind chutney onto it. Garnish with sev, coriander leaves, julienne carrot and beetroot and sprinkle the chaat masala and roasted cumin powder over it. You are all set to enjoy this delicious chaat recipe.
