Sweet Potato Recipes

Description

Sweet Potato Chaat is a sweet tangy chaat topped with pomegranates and freshly chopped coriander. This chaat can go well with any season but it's mostly enjoyed in almost every season

If you love sweet potatoes, then this chaat can be a great counterpart for your normal aloo chaat. Sweet Potato Chaat like other street food chaats is filled with spices and chutney. So, all the street food lovers, this is your time to relish this delicious chaat at the comfort pf your home. Do try making this chaat and let's know if you enjoyed it.

Ingredients

  • 300 gm chopped,boiled,peeled sweet potato
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala powder
  • black salt as required
  • salt as required
  • pomegranate seeds as required
  • 1 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoon tamrind chutney
  • 1 handful chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Add sweet potatoes in a bowl

    2. To make this dish, boil and peel the sweet potatoes. Next, take a bowl, add sweet potatoes and cumin powder, salt, chaat masala, black salt, and sugar. Mix everything well.

  2. Pour in tamarind chutney

    3. Once the potatoes are mixed well with powdered spices, add in tamarind chutney. Mix to combine well.

  3. Garnish with pomegranate seeds

    4. Now, pour the prepared chaat into a serving bowl and top it with pomegranate seeds.

  4. Your Sweet Potato Chaat is ready to serve

    5. Season it with lemon juice and freshly chopped coriander leaves. Give a nice stir and it's done. Your Sweet Potato Chaat is ready. Enjoy.
